HOW TO PLAY: Click on any of the grid or boxes to start playing. The game is played by two players (You and a Computer). If you clicked on any of the boxes the value X will be placed at the position, almost immediately the computer will respond by placing O on any of the empty boxes. You can hit the restart button to play another game after a game has ended.
